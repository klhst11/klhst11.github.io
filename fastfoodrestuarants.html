My Favortite Fast Food Restuarants:

Chick-Fil-A

I love Chick-Fil-A because of their chicken. Their chicken is always very tasty and is juicy. I personally love to get their nuggets and chick-fil-a sauce everytime I go there, along with their lemonade because it is sweet and tastes very good. Definitely my favorite place to go.

Taco Bell

I love Taco Bell because of their $5, $6, or $7 boxes. Every 1 or 2 months, their variety changes with these boxes, with different kinds of foods. It usually is tacos, but everytime a new one comes out, they offer different kinds of tacos in it. Currently they have a nacho fry box, which is good. I remember other times they had a dorito taco box, as well as a grilled cheese type of taco box. It also includes a drink.

Wendy's

I love Wendy's because of their chicken nuggets and $5 biggie bag. My favorite thing to get there is chicken nuggets, but I do most of the time get their $5 bag because it comes with a drink, burger, fries, and 4 nuggets. To me that is a great deal for $5, and it is great food overall.

Burger King

I love Burger king because of their burgers and onion rings. Their onion rings have always been my favorite, but their burgers I feel have gotten a lot better as well. I really like their whoppers and a bbq burger they have. They also have a ranch burger which includes 2 onion rings, bbq sauce, cheese, and a burger, which is perfect for everything that I like.
